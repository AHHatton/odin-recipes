Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are super easy to make. this is the recipe from the back of the Tollhouse bag.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Mix all the wet ingredients together
  2. Add the flour and mix until combined
  3. Add the chocolate chips
  4. Taste test, you're not a monster
  5. Plop onto a tray and bake at 350 for 9-14 minutes
  6. If you burn them, serve them to your husband
