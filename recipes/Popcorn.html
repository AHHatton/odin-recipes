Popcorn

Description

If you want to make your house smell good, make popcorn. It will also make your dog come out of their hidey-hole so they can beg more effectively.

Ingredients

  • Popping corn
  • Veg oil
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Other seasonings (optional)

Steps

  1. Put 3 kernels of popping corn in the middle of a small oil puddle in a pan on the stovetop
  2. Heat on medium with the lid on
  3. Once these kernels start to pop, add the rest of the poping corn and lower the heat
  4. Swirl and shake like it's your only job until the rest pop
  5. Transfer to a bowl
  6. Melt some butter and pour over the popcorn
  7. Add salt and any other seasonings you like
